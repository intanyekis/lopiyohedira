Katy Perry Has New $1000 Grill

Katy Perry was roaring and looking fierce when she arrived at the red carpet of the recently concluded MTV Video Music Awards wearing multi-colored grills.

The “Roar” singer sported diamond-encrusted grills worth $1,000 from mouthpiece jeweler Paul Wall. The title of her new single was also etched into it. Perry has joined the likes of Madonna, Miley Cyrus and Rihanna who also flashed their grills in the past.

Perry complemented her grills by donning a thigh slit leopard-print dress showing a little of her sexy legs coupled with simple black heels. She was not nominated in any of the categories but made an astounding performance, causing the audience to roar as she closed the 2013 VMA ceremony on Sunday night in Brooklyn.

The 28-year-old “I Kissed a Girl” hitmaker has a net worth of $75 million. Her new single "Roar", that came from her upcoming album Prism which is to be released this October, is considered the biggest-selling digital song of 2013.
