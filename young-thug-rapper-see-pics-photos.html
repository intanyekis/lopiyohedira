See Photos Of Rapper Hollywood Life

Young Thug attends the 3rd Annual Diamond Ball at Cipriani Wall Street, in New York, NY in 2017. He looked great in glasses.

Young Thug is best known as a rapper. He first broke into the hip hop music scene after signing with Gucci Mane’s 1017 Records in 2013. He released a debut mixtape that year and went on to release the singles “Stoner” and “Danny Glover” in 2014. He also appeared on several other singles, including T.I.’s “About the Money”, Tyga’s “Hookah”, and Rich Gang’s “Lifestyle” and released other mixtapes over the years. His debut studio album, ‘So Much Fun’, was eventually released in 2019.

He has won several awards for his work and is involved in philanthropic work as well. He has joined campaigns to fight for poverty and once donated all proceeds from his sold out concert to Planned Parenthood.
