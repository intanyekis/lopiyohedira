Hiccup Haddock: Is The Protagonist From HTTYD Trans?

The creators of the HTTYD have not explicitly addressed the fact that the protagonist of the movie, Hiccup, voiced by Jay Baruchel, is trans. However, some viewers have recognized and suggested that the film’s and sequels’ tale serves as a sympathetic trans allegory.

Several Twitter users have expressed their belief that Hiccup is transgender. “Hiccup’s tale in the first movie certainly isn’t a trans allegory, but there are so many similarities if you think about it,” one user, Joni, trans bean, said.

The first film opens with Hiccup making his best effort to fit in and assimilate with those around him. He also learns about the dragon’s behavior.

Hiccup Haddock: How Did He Lose His Leg?

Netizens are keen to know how the protagonist from How To Train Your Dragon, Hiccup Haddock, lost his leg. In the first film, Hiccup slips off the saddle and into the fire after he and Toothless fought the Red Death and the dragon explodes.

Toothless dives after him and saves him. There are two versions of what occurred. The first version is that Toothless becomes unable to defend Hiccup’s entire body from the flames, resulting in his leg being burned.

The other one is that to reach Hiccup, Toothless had to grab his leg, which he ended up hurting so badly that they had to chop it off.
