Hailey Bieber Enjoys Getaway With Husband Justin Bieber: Pics

Warm in the winter! Hailey Bieber (née Baldwin) spent the holidays with husband Justin Bieber and some of her closest pals, including Kylie Jenner and Stassie Karanikolaou.

The supermodel, 26, shared a series of snaps from the end of 2022 via Instagram on Monday, January 2. “Holiday Dump ❄️☀️,” the Rhode founder captioned the photos. “Best times. best friends. Happy New Year ✨✨.”

In one photo, Hailey and her husband, 28, cuddled up on the snowy slopes during their getaway to Aspen, Colorado. The picture showed the Arizona native sitting on Justin’s lap as they both kept warm in heavy coats and boots.

Hailey also shared a video of herself dancing to Tame Impala’s “The Less I Know the Better” while wearing a headband reading “Happy New Year.” In another clip, she trudged through snow piles that went up to her knees.

Jenner and Karanikolaou, both 25, also gave fans a glimpse of the relaxing trip, posting photos from the vacation via their own Instagram accounts. “2023,” the Kylie Cosmetics founder captioned her carousel, which included a snap of her 4-year-old daughter, Stormi, looking at deer through a car window. In another photo, the Hulu personality gave her BFF a kiss on the cheek while Hailey stood nearby in her winter gear.

“Bae ❤️,” Karanikolaou replied in the comments section of Jenner’s post. On her own Instagram feed, the influencer shared a selfie in ski goggles as well as a candid shot of her and Hailey dolled up for New Year’s Eve.

Hailey has been friends with Karanikolaou and the Life of Kylie alum for years. “[Hailey] was actually best friends with Kylie, and I was good friends with her cousin Ireland [Baldwin],” Kendall Jenner revealed in a March 2021 interview on Hailey’s YouTube series, explaining that she grew closer to her fellow model because they worked in the same industry.

The 818 Tequila founder, 27, added that her friendship with Hailey initially caused a bit of friction with Kylie, who knew Hailey before Kendall did. “At first, Kylie was mad,” Kendall explained. “I had to bring them back together and be like, ‘No, it’s OK, guys.’ But it’s all good, that’s our love story.”
