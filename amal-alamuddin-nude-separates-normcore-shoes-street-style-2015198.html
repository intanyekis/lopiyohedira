Amal Alamuddin Rocks Nude Separates With Normcore Shoes: Street Style

Verdict: Amal Alamuddin continues to slay the street style scene!

Case in point, the accomplished international human rights lawyer and wife to George Clooney, 37, stepped out in London on Monday, Aug. 17, working an ensemble we can't stop obsessing over: nude Stella McCartney separates coupled with a top-handle Les Petits Joueurs Lego bag and—wait for it—normcore buckled shoes.

It was a bold and unexpected look from Alamuddin, who's well-known for her love of '70s style, not to mention tailored suits. But this is the first time we've spotted her working a '90s staple into her wardrobe.
