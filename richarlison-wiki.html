Richarlison Wiki- Early Life/Career

Early Life & Career: - Richarlison made his professional debut on July 4, 2015, against Mogi Mirim in a 3-1 home win. He scored the game's last goal after coming on as a late substitution for Cristiano. He renewed his contract till 2018 seventeen days later. Richarlison was sent off at the conclusion of the match on November 21, 2015, as América gained promotion to Série A with a 1-1 home draw against Ceará.

Richarlison was named to Brazil's under-20 team for the South American U-20 Championship in 2017. During the competition, he appeared in eight games and scored two goals. Coach Tite called him up to the senior squad for friendlies against the United States and El Salvador on August 27, 2018, after Pedro withdrew due to injury.
