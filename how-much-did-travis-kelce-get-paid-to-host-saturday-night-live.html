Who is Travis Kelce?

Travis Kelce is a professional American football player who plays as a tight end for the Kansas City Chiefs in the National Football League (NFL). He was born in October 1989, in Westlake, Ohio, and attended the University of Cincinnati, where he played college football for the Cincinnati Bearcats.

Kelce was selected by the Kansas City Chiefs in the third round of the 2013 NFL Draft and has since become one of the top tight ends in the league.

He has been named to the Pro Bowl six times, received First-Team All-Pro honours twice, and helped lead the Chiefs to two Super Bowl appearances, including a victory in Super Bowl LIV.

In addition to his success on the football field, Kelce has become known for his off-the-field endeavours, including hosting his own reality dating show, Catching Kelce. Most recently, he added Saturday Night Live host to his list of accomplishments when he appeared on the show in March 2023.
