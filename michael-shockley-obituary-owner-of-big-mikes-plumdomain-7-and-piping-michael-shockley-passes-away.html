Michael Shockley Obituary: Owner of Big Mikes Plumbing and Piping, Michael Shockley passes away

Michael Shockley Rogersville, AL Obituary – Cause of Death: Owner of Big Mike’s Plumbing and Piping, Michael Shockley passes away.

On Friday, October 13, 2023, Big Mike’s Plumbing and Piping announced the recent passing of owner Michael Shockley via a statement shared on social media.

“I am writing with my deepest regrets to announce the recent passing of Michael Shockley, owner of Big Mike’s Plumbing and Piping,” Big Mike’s Plumbing and Piping announced.

Mike’s cause of death was not mentioned.

“Owning his own plumbing business was a dream Mike had for a great portion of his life, and thanks to you, his customers and friends, he was able to make it a reality. Without your support and business, this would have never been possible.”

According to the business, there will be a celebration of life for Mike on Monday October 16th. Visitation: 3-5PM. Graveside: 5PM. He will be deeply missed by his wife, children, family, and friends.
