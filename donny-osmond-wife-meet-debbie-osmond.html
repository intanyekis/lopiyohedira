Donny Osmond Wife: Meet Debbie Osmond

Debbie Osmond is the wife of Donny Osmond an American singer, dancer, actor, television host and former teen idol.

The couple has known each other since they were teenagers. Osmond and his now-wife first met when he was 17 and she was 15.

Debbie, a high school cheerleader, was dating Jay, one of the Osmond brothers, at the time. Osmond persuaded Debbie to marry him after three years of courtship, and the couple married in May 1978.

Together they have five sons: Don, Jeremy, Brandon, Christopher, and Joshua. The Osmonds became grandparents in 2005 and have 13 grandchildren.

Debbie has appeared on shows such as Martha Stewart, Rachael Ray, and Dancing with the Stars though not as a contestant. Debbie’s current interests include home improvement projects, cooking, and interior design. She and her husband currently run the Donny Osmond Home and Coaster Fine Furniture furniture lines.

Donny Osmond revealed to PEOPLE about how his wife Debbie Osmond has been his biggest source of support as he struggled with crippling anxiety, career blockages, and near-bankruptcy for years.

Speaking about his wife of 44 years, Osmond stated: “Debbie has given my life stability. She keeps my feet on the ground.”
