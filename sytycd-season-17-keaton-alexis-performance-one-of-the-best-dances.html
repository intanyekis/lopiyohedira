'SYTYCD' Season 17: Keaton and Alexis' act was 'one of the best dances' in show's history, say fans

Season 17 of 'So You Think You Can Dance' is finally here and for better or for worse, the show has been making waves. While everyone seems to have an opinion about the new format, at the end of the day, the show hopes to give dancers the platform they deserve. With the choreography round done, it is now finally time for the top 12 to take center stage!
