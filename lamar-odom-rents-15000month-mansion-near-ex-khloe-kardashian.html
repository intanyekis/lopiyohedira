Lamar Odom Rents $15,000/Month Mansion Near Ex Khloe Kardashian

Lamar Odom and Khloe Kardashian may be on amicable terms after the NBA star signed a lease on a mansion suspiciously close to his ex-wife.

According to TMZ, Lamar is relocating to Calabasas, California after living in Atlanta full-time. He’s reportedly renting out a mansion in the area for $15,000 per month. He signed a 1-year lease, even though Calabasas is the known stomping ground of the Kardashian family.

With over 4,445 square feet of living space, the mansion offers a ton of luxury amenities, including a built-in BBQ and outdoor spa with waterfalls. The common area also comes with a gym, tennis court, and clubhouse.

Lamar and Khloe married in a televised ceremony in 2009, only a month after meeting. However, she filed for divorce in 2013 amid allegations of substance abuse and infidelity.

Nevertheless, the couple weren’t officially divorced until 2016. In fact, Khloe temporarily paused the proceedings in 2015 after Lamar overdosed at a Nevada brothel and was hospitalized.

At the time, there was speculation the pair were reconciling. But Khloe denied the rumors, later saying she only withdrew the divorce proceedings so she could make medical decisions on his behalf. She later re-filed, and the divorce was granted a year later.

It's unclear if Lamar and Khloe are in contact following their divorce. But he hasn’t shied away from commenting on her romantic life, particularly her on-and-off relationship with Tristan Thompson.

Khloe began dating Tristan in 2016, and they welcomed a daughter, True, in 2018. However, Tristan was exposed for cheating on Khloe days before their daughter’s birth.

The NBA player has been involved in several other cheating scandals in recent years, though he and Khloe tried to reconcile multiple times.

Most recently, he was exposed for fathering a child with another woman while dating Khloe Kardashian last year. He and Khloe broke up, only to announce in mid-2022 they were expecting a second child via surrogate.

Khloe’s representative claimed the reality star was unaware of Tristan’s other child when the surrogate became pregnant. The pair are apparently not together, despite welcoming a baby boy in the summer.

In July, Lamar admitted that he would’ve helped Khloe have another baby if she asked.

“Oh, [Tristan] got caught cheating again? And they’re gonna have another baby?” he told Page Six. “She could have hollered at me for that.”

Khloe never publicly responded to Lamar’s comments.
