'Nic has bankrolled against me'

Hila acknowledges in court filings that the Cage wedding debacle is "out of the scope" of the financial assistance request she made on January 30, 2023, but she only wanted to show to the Court how the Cage family treated her before, during, and after her early 2021 separation from Weston.
