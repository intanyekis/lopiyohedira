Breaking News: Cody Rhodes Declares Himself Leader Of Bullet Club Bullet Club Is Fine. Bullet Clu

After a blistering tag match against each other in Korakuen Hall, Bullet Club members Cody Rhodes and Kenny Omega got into a ringside brawl. The Young Bucks and various members of Bullet Club pulled them apart, but the damage was clearly done. Cody took to the microphone to address the recent tensions in Bullet Club.

He said that, by losing, The Golden Lovers reunion “ended before it began.” He then went on to say that Kota Ibushi was “Kenny Omega’s rat.” After the derogatory remark, Cody went on to call himself the “face of Ring of Honor.” Cody said that everyone was either “Team Cody or Team Kenny.” He then ended his speech by saying “Bullet Club is fine. Bullet Club is mine.”

This is the first time that either Rhodes or Omega has claimed leadership over the seemingly autonomous group. This also suggests that Kenny Omega is no longer in Bullet Club.
