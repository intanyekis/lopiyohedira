Mark Ballas is an American dancer, choreographer, singer, songwriter, musician, actor, and television personality who has been a professional ballroom dancer on the ABC show Dancing with the Stars. He was nominated for an Emmy for “Outstanding Choreography” in 2011. As a musician, he has been a member of the duo Alexander Jean, alongside BC Jean. The duo has released extended plays and tracks like For Anybody Wondering, So Bad, Head High, Coming Down, Roses and Violets, High Enough, O Holy Night, Stampede (ft. Lindsey Stirling), Paper Planes, Highs & Lows, Waiting for You, We Three Kings (ft. Casey Abrams), Nevermind, and God Rest You Merry, Gentlemen.
