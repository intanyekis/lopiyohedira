Princess Diana, the People's Princess, gained recognition not only for her humanitarian work and compassionate nature but also for her impeccable sense of style. Her jewelry collection testified to her elegance and grace, featuring some of the world's most exquisite and valuable pieces. This article explores nine of the most expensive and iconic jewelry pieces that Princess Diana owned, delving into the history and significance of each piece.

From engagement rings to tiaras, these stunning pieces of jewelry adorned Princess Diana and captured the hearts of millions worldwide.

The Asprey Aquamarine Ring - $98,000

This stunning ring features a large emerald-cut aquamarine stone, surrounded by diamonds and set in 18-carat yellow gold. Princess Diana received the Aquamarine as a gift from her friend, Lucia Flecha de Lima, and was made into a ring after her divorce in 1996. She was often seen wearing the ring at various public events. Prince Harry inherited the ring upon Diana’s death, and Megan Markle uses it. The ring's estimated value is $98,000, making it one of the most valuable pieces in Princess Diana’s collection.

The 11-Strand Pearl Choker - $250,000

This exquisite choker comprises 11 strands consisting of 900 pearls, held together by thin columns of diamonds and rubies, according to Crisscut Magazine. Princess Diana often wore this piece to formal events, showcasing her love for pearls. The choker's estimated value is $250,000, making it a significant part of her jewelry collection.

The Spencer Family Tiara - $500,000

Gifted to Princess Diana’s grandmother, Countess Cynthia Spencer, in 1919., the Spencer Family Tiara is a stunning piece of work that has been in the Spencer family for generations. Princess Diana wore this tiara on her wedding day and other important occasions. Made of gold and silver, the tiara features an intricate design of tulips, star-shaped flowers, and scrolling foliage, all adorned with diamonds. The estimated value of this family heirloom is $500,000, and it currently belongs to Diana’s brother, Charles Spencer.

The Sapphire and Diamond Engagement Ring - $675,000

Perhaps the most famous piece of jewelry in Princess Diana's collection is her engagement ring, which now belongs to her daughter-in-law, Kate Middleton. The ring features a 12-carat oval Ceylon sapphire, surrounded by 25 solitaire diamonds and set in 18-carat white gold, according to 1 Trove. Valued at a whopping $675,000, this iconic ring symbolized the love and commitment between Princess Diana and Prince Charles at one time.

The Queen Mary's Lover's Knot Tiara - $1.2 Million

This iconic tiara, also known as the Cambridge Lover's Knot, was a favorite of Princess Diana and was given to her by Queen Elizabeth as a wedding gift. According to She Knows, the tiara was originally made for Queen Mary in 1914 and featured a Gothic Revival style, flaunting a beautiful ensemble of pearls and diamonds. The tiara's estimated value is around a massive $1.2 million, and it made its last public appearance in 2018 when Kate Middleton wore it to the State Banquet for King Willem-Alexander of the Netherlands.

The Swan Lake Diamond And Pearl Necklace - $12.1 Million

Garrard, the official jeweler of the British royal family, designed this breathtaking necklace. The necklace features a stunning arrangement of 178 diamonds and pearls inspired by the ballet Swan Lake. Princess Diana wore this piece to a ballet performance in 1997, just months before her tragic death. The necklaces' estimated value is around a staggering $12.1 million.

The Delhi Durbar Emerald Choker - $12.5 Million

Originally part of Queen Mary's Delhi Durbar parure in 1911, as per Veranda, this exquisite choker features a large emerald surrounded by diamonds. Princess Diana received the choker as a wedding gift from Queen Elizabeth II and often wore it as a headband. The choker's estimated value is $12.5 million, making it one of the most expensive pieces in Princess Diana’s collection.

The Saudi Sapphire-and-Diamond Jewelry Suite - $25.1 Million

This stunning suite of jewelry was a wedding gift from the Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia. The suite includes a necklace, earrings, bracelet, and ring, all featuring sapphires and diamonds. Princess Diana wore these pieces numerous times, showcasing their beauty and elegance, and further redesigned the necklace as a headband featuring a large deep blue Burmese sapphire pendant. The estimated value of the suite stands at a gigantic $25.1 million.

The Queen Mother's Sapphire Brooch - $125 Million

This stunning brooch, originally from the Queen Mother, features a large sapphire surrounded by diamonds. Princess Diana redesigned the brooch as a pendant on a seven-strand pearl choker. She wore it on several occasions, including in 1985 during her dance with John Travolta at the White House and in 1994, pairing it with her infamous ’revenge dress’ after Prince Charles admitted to his affair. The Sapphire brooch is the most expensive jewelry piece on this list, estimated at more than $125 Million.

Princess Diana's jewelry collection is a testament to her timeless elegance and refined taste. Each piece holds a unique story and significance, reflecting the life and legacy of the People's Princess. These jewelry pieces, valued at millions of dollars, are a part of history and a symbol of Princess Diana's enduring impact on the world.
