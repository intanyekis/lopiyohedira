Highlights

Miley Cyrus has three brothers and two sisters, and it must have been interesting for them to grow up with a child star in the family. Ever since she starred in Hannah Montana, Miley's career has absolutely taken off. It seems like the world is her oyster, whether she wants to release more albums or star in new movies.

Although Miley Cyrus' siblings also have money, there's no denying the fact that she has the highest net worth because of her acting and music careers. While Miley is of course the most well-known out of the Cyrus siblings, her sister Noah is in the public eye a fair amount. Miley's 36-year-old half-sister Brandi is quieter, however. Here's what Brandi Cyrus does for work and what she really thinks about her celebrity sister.

What Does Miley Cyrus' Sister Brandi Cyrus Do For A Living?

Tish Cyrus was married to her first husband Baxter Neal Hudson from 1986 to 1989. In 1987, the couple became parents to Miley Cyrus's half-sister Brandi Cyrus. According to People, Billy Ray Cyrus became Brandi's adoptive dad in 1993.

According to a profile of Miley Cyrus' sister Brandi Cyrus in The House Magazine, Brandi podcasts for a living. She and Bachelor in Paradise bartender Wells Adams co-host You're Favorite Thing. Brandi and Wells Adams chat about various subjects, from travel to pop culture. Adams's wife Sarah Hyland often drops by the podcast as well.

Brandi and her mother Tish Cyrus also co-host the podcast Sorry, We're Stoned. The two chat about their lives and there are lots of juicy, fun details in every episode. A few recent episodes focus on Tish's 2023 wedding to Dominic Purcell and honeymoon.

Miley Cyrus' sister Brandi Cyrus also works as a DJ. According to The House Magazine, she has some downtime at the beginning of every year, and she loves to go horseback riding.

Brandi's job as a DJ sees her traveling all over. She told the publication, “Now that I’m DJing and playing these bigger clubs where house music is expected, I started to really dive into it and figure out what house and electronic music I enjoy. When I play these remixes in the club, there is a vibe for sure. It’s fun to mix electronic music with the hip-hop stuff that I love because there is a way to do both. To me, that’s the creative art of DJing… making it all work together.”

In October 2023, Brand will DJ an event called The Villains' Ball ft. Brandi Cyrus x Nathan Leong.

Brandi Cyrus is also an actress. While she played a few minor roles in Hannah Montana and The Hannah Montana Movie, she also appeared in the 2015 horror movie Old 37.

Brandi has had a lot of different creative jobs. In 2017, she and Tish Cyrus co-hosted the Bravo series Cyrus vs. Cyrus: Design and Conquer. In every episode, they helped people with a cool redesign.

In an interview with Yahoo!, Brandi shared that she hasn't always felt super confident when beginning a new professional path. However, she didn't let that stop her, which is inspiring. She said, “When I started I had no idea what I was doing. I would take jobs and just throw myself into it and kind of learn as I went. It was scary! With my career, every time I've done something that I was afraid to do or felt I was unqualified for, I've always been so grateful and thankful that I did it.”

How Does Brandi Cyrus Feel About Her Sister Miley Cyrus?

While some might expect Miley Cyrus' sister to be envious of her wealth and place in Hollywood, that doesn't appear to be the case at all. Brandi has always been very supportive of Miley, and the two seem to have a good relationship.

According to Entertainment Tonight, Brandi Cyrus talked about her sister in a compassionate manner during a 2019 episode of the You're My Favorite Thing podcast. She said of Miley and Liam Hemsworth's split, "It's not my business to talk about and it's nothing anything of mine to tell. There's nothing I can really say. I just, I'm here for her, obviously, and I've been spending a lot of time with her. When she's ready to talk about it or whatever, then she will."

Brandi has always sounded positive when speaking about her father and siblings as well.

In 2015, Brandi Cyrus talked about her family with Entertainment Tonight. When asked what people get wrong about the Cyruses, she said, "For some reason, people love to hate a Cyrus. I don't know what it is, but I remember when I was a kid, and my dad [Billy Ray Cyrus] was at the height of his success, all people wanted to do was make fun of me for it."

She added to Entertainment Tonight, "I was like, 'Wait a minute. My dad is the biggest star on the planet and you want to make fun of me?' As I'm older now, I look back on that and that's just so insane. I don't know why people like to tear others down to make themselves feel better. I don't know what it is but, yes, Miley is a huge personality. She's a weirdo, but she's so normal."

Brandi Cyrus definitely doesn't resent her sister Miley Cyrus for being super famous. Instead, it sounds like Brandi is very happy with the way that she grew up, and she appreciates everything that their father Billy Ray Cyrus did for them.

According to Horseandhound.co.uk, Brandi began horseback riding at six, and she has been thrilled to continue with that passion since. Brandi said that after Billy Ray Cyrus became wealthy, he purchased Tennessee property, which meant that she lived on a farm and learned to ride horses.

She continues to ride today. As she told Fashionlush.com, "I have several hour long workouts saved on my computer so I can do them anywhere – and it doesn’t hurt to have a hobby that demands some athleticism. I ride horses 6 days a week when i’m home, and it’s a full body workout but I don’t notice because I’m having fun."
