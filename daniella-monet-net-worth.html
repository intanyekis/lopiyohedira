Daniella Monet

Birthname Daniella Monet Zuvic Birthdate March 1, 1989 Birthplace West Hills, California Source of Wealth acting, dancing, singing Nationality American Country United States Marital Status Single

Daniella Monet is an American actress, singer and dancer.

Daniella Monet first guest starred on an episode of Pacific Blue when she was seven years old. She then appeared in several television series, including American Dreams and The Bernie Mac Show, in 2003.

She landed a recurring role on the television series 8 Simple Rules, playing Missy Keinfield.

From 2006-2007, she appeared in three episodes of Zoey 101. She also appeared in the 2006 film Simon Says and in the 2007 films Nancy Drew and Taking 5. From 2010-2013, Monet starred in the Nickelodeon sitcom, Victorious, playing Trina Vega.

Monet also hosted Nickelodeon show, AwesomenessTV.

Daniella Monet Zuvic was born on March 1, 1989 in West Hills, California, USA. Aside from being active in her career on television, she is an animal welfare advocate and a vegan.
