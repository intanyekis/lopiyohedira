Julian Morris, Pretty Little Liars Hunk, Naked in Wonderland Photos

Toto, I don’t think we’re in Rosewood anymore. ABC Family hunk Julian Morris, who plays Wren on Pretty Little Liars, shed his inhibitions — and all of his clothes — for a recent photo spread in the U.K.’s Wonderland magazine. The 30-year-old actor appears completely naked in the steamy pictures, save for a strategically placed towel or two.

In one shot, Morris angles his body away from the camera to reveal a glimpse of his bare butt. In another, he stands with his back against a white wall, totally nude except for a white towel that just barely covers his nether regions.

Speaking with the mag in an accompanying interview, the London-born star opens up about his career, his 30th birthday, and his favorite pastime. On the subject of the latter, he says: “I love sex. I love having sex.”

Morris certainly knows how to let loose. “I’ve got an awesome group of people around me who I love, they’re my family,” he tells Wonderland. “When I turned 30 I rented a place on the North shore of Maui, and on the day of my birthday we went to the national park, to this freshwater cave system…and then kind of made it into a Bjork video with glow sticks and tequila and music.”
